Cookie Dough

This edible cookie dough recipe is egg-free and will satisfy any cravings for chocolate chip cookies without the wait.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Place flour in microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute 15 seconds, stirirng every 15 seconds
  2. Beat sugar and butter in large bowl until creamy. Beat vanilla extract and salt.
  3. Add heat treated flour
  4. Mix until crumbly dough forms.
  5. Stir in milk until dough is combined
  6. Fold in chocolate chips
